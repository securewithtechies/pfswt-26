# Thank Our Sponsors

**Category:** Welcome  
**Points:** 10  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Challenge Description

Show some appreciation for the sponsors who made this event possible!

---

## Solution

This is a simple appreciation challenge for the event sponsors.

The flag is embedded in the sponsors section or related content.

**Flag:** `pfswt{thank_you_sponsors}`

---

## How to Find

1. Check the sponsors page on the CTF platform
2. Look at the event website's sponsor section
3. Read through sponsor acknowledgments in the event materials

---

## Purpose

This challenge serves to:
- Draw attention to the sponsors who support the event
- Provide an easy flag for participation points
- Encourage participants to explore the platform
