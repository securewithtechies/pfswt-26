# Social Connect

**Category:** Welcome  
**Points:** 10  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Challenge Description

Connect with us on social media to find the hidden flag!

---

## Solution

This challenge requires visiting the organizer's social media accounts.

### Steps

1. **Find the official Twitter/X handle:** @SecureWithTech
2. **Look for a post** related to the CTF
3. **The flag is in the post** or its comments

### Location
The flag can be found on one of these platforms:
- **Twitter/X:** Pinned tweet or comment
- **Instagram:** Bio or pinned story
- **Discord:** #welcome channel
- **LinkedIn:** Featured post
- **Telegram:** Pinned message

**Flag:** `pfswt{f0ll0w_us_4_m0r3_ctfs}`

## Purpose

This challenge:
- Builds community engagement
- Helps grow the organizer's social media presence
- Introduces participants to channels for future event announcements
- Provides networking opportunities within the cybersecurity community

## Tip

Always follow CTF organizers on social media - they often post:
- Early announcements for future events
- Hints during ongoing CTFs
- Job opportunities and resources
- Community discussions
