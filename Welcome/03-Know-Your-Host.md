# Know Your Host

**Category:** Welcome  
**Points:** 10  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Challenge Description

Who is organizing this CTF? Find the name of the organization hosting Phantom Flags CTF.

---

## Solution

The CTF is organized by **Secure With Techies (SWT)**.

### How to Find

1. **Check the CTF platform's about section** - Usually contains organizer information
2. **Look at the flag prefix** - `pfswt` = **P**hantom **F**lags **S**ecure **W**ith **T**echies
3. **Check social media handles** - Event announcements mention the organizer
4. **Read the event rules** - Organizer name is typically mentioned

### Flag Construction
- Organization name: Secure With Techies
- Format: lowercase with underscores

**Flag:** `pfswt{secure_with_techies}`

---

## Learning Points

1. Always research the CTF organizers - they often leave hints in their branding
2. Flag prefixes sometimes encode meaningful information
3. Social media and official channels are valuable information sources
